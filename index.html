Dice Imperium

March your army around the world. Land on a territory and seize it — but every conquest is settled by the dice.

How to win: control 3 complete continents (or the whole map).
Roll two dice and march. Land on free land to garrison it, on enemy land to invade — attacker rolls up to 3 dice, defender up to 2, highest dice win, ties favor the defender. Pass Command HQ to collect 5 fresh troops plus a bonus for every continent you fully control. Doubles roll again; three doubles lands you in Quarantine.
